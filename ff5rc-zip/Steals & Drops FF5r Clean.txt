FF5r Steals and Drops
Compiled by KainStryder
-----------------------

Spoilers for every enemy in the game follow.

Enemies can have up to two rare and one common steal.
The second rare steal is the same item as the rare drop.

You can steal from an enemy until they have no items left.
This means you can get every item from an enemy.

If an enemy has a "Common Drop", it will always drop.
